Channels Choose what you need

Google Calendar

With GCalendar channel has two triggers, and an action and are based on calendar events associated with your Gmail account.

GMail

With GMail channel you can only have one trigger and a single action. The trigger is based on the receipt of emails from your Gmail account, and operates according to the object of the email, or to a specific chosen address..

Twitter

With Twittwer channel there are two actions that allow you to automatically publish Tweets and two triggers which watch special events such as receiving a private message or posting a Tweet by a particular person. You must logged in with you Twitter account to continue.

Weather

In this channel you can choose four different triggers that can be triggered according to specific weather events, such as the sunset hour or when the current temperature is within a specified range.
